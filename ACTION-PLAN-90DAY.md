## Why This Plan Works

1. **You've sold face-to-face before** → We leverage that strength (warm outreach, relationships)
2. **One customer = everything changes** → Once you deliver, proof compounds (case study, review, referral)
3. **Referrals are free leads** → Customer #1 becomes your best salesperson
4. **Partnerships are warm channels** → A single partnership = 1–2 leads/month without your effort
5. **You're at zero** → Anything you build compounds. First customer is the hardest. Second is easier.

90 days to prove the model. Then scale.
