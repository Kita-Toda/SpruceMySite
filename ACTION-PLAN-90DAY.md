## The Problem We're Solving
Currently: 0 customers, lots of preparation work (SEO, content, design).  
Reality: You don't need perfect SEO yet. You need ONE customer. Everything else scales from there.

**Strategy:** Get first customer → deliver brilliantly → build proof + systems → scale via referrals + partnerships.

## Phase 1: Customer Acquisition (Days 1–30)

**Goal:** One signed contract by Day 30.

### Week 1: Launch & Outreach List (Days 1–7)

| Task | Deadline | Owner | Success Metric |

## Why This Plan Works

1. **You've sold face-to-face before** → We leverage that strength (warm outreach, relationships)
2. **One customer = everything changes** → Once you deliver, proof compounds (case study, review, referral)
3. **Referrals are free leads** → Customer #1 becomes your best salesperson
4. **Partnerships are warm channels** → A single partnership = 1–2 leads/month without your effort
5. **You're at zero** → Anything you build compounds. First customer is the hardest. Second is easier.
